

======
 ANTS
======

Studying Collective Foraging strategy:
Though individual ants adopt erratic paths to look for food,
the ant colony may discover optimal paths to get to food 
and exploit food sources.

Press <Space> to pause the simulation
Press <Esc> or <Q> to quit the simulation



=================
 Segregationism
=================

Though individual agents show only slight preference for being surrounded by similar agent,
homogeneous patches emerge.

Press <Space> to pause the simulation
Press <Esc> or <Q> to quit the simulation
